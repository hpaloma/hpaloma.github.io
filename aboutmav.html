Mavrik Joos aka Mav

digital creator, blogger, camper

Maverick Joos is an Internet personality, known for his camping content on YouTube. He was born in December 1995 in Duluth, Minnesota. Not only does he post camping media, but he also sells merch on his personal website. From trekking the Rocky Mountains, to truck camping in the suburbs, Mavrik Joos offers a variety of camping content. This includes sharing recipes, tips, camping gear, and more! Follow Mav on Youtube: @Mav to see him in action.
