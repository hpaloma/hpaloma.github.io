Beginner: The Reuben

  • Rye bread, two slices
  • Corned beef (prepackaged or homemade)
    approx. three ounces
  • Swiss cheese, two slices
  • Sauerkraut - any jarred brand you like!
    approx. three tablespoons
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Thousand Island Dressing : bottled or mixed together.
    (Equal parts mayo, ketchup, and relish)
  1. Toast two slices of rye bread in a hot cast iron skillet,
    with olive oil or butter.
  2. Pile slices of corned beef and sauerkraut onto one slice of bread,
    and add slices of swiss cheese! (Trick to melt the cheese? Add a lid!)
  3. Toast it golden brown.
  4. Spoon dressing into the sandwich once cooked.
  5. “Cut it diagonally - it just feels better.”

Advanced: Buffalo Mac and Cheese

  • Boneless/skinless chicken breast
  • 1/3 cup blue cheese dressing
  • 8 oz. cavatappi pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 Tablespoon tomato paste
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup buffalo sauce
  • ½ teaspoon hot sauce
  • ½ teaspoon mustard powder
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon each: salt/pepper
  • 1 ¼ cups cheddar cheese
  • 2 ounces cream cheese
  1. Submerge the chicken in a pot of water and gradually bring it to a gentle boil. Cook for 15-20 minutes, until chicken is cooked through.
  2. Remove the chicken and use 2 forks to shred it. Toss with blue cheese dressing and set aside.
  3. Begin boiling salted water in a large pot while you start the sauce. Once a boil is reached, add the pasta and cook until al dente according to package instructions. (Don’t overcook.) Drain and set aside.
  4. Melt the butter in a pot over medium heat. Add the flour and stir continuously for 1 minute. Add the tomato paste and stir to incorporate, you’ll be left with a thick paste.
  5. Add the heavy cream and milk in small splashes, stirring continuously. Doing this slowly ensures that the sauce stays thick.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  7. Add the buffalo sauce, hot sauce, mustard powder, onion powder, and salt/pepper.
  8. Gradually sprinkle in the cheddar cheese and cream cheese, stirring continuously.
  9. Stir in the shredded chicken, then add the drained pasta. Use a silicone spatula to gently stir to combine. Serve!
