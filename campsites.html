Today's Feature: Japan

Maverick visited Japan and posted content from it in March 2023. His first night in Japan was spent in Fukui, a somewhat woodsy and open camp area on the water. He then spent that night in a rainy Toyama, and traveled to Okishima the next day. His next camp spot was the icy Takayama, surrounded by the woods and chunks of ice. Next stop was a camp site in Noboribetsu, which was nothing too special other than a parking lot. Lastly, he went to Teshikaga, where he enjoyed snowmobiling on the frozen lake!
